Yuri Akabane

Stories of the land of The Blazing Sun had always been heard. A place where other gods were worshipped, and where forests were always red or adorned with cherry trees. It was a forbidden land. It is said to be protected by an impenetrable wall of fog from which no one ever returned.

However, those stories existed for a reason: Occasionally, whispers were heard of people from that place wandering through the Realms of Fae'Gir.

Yuri was one of them.

A samurai without a lord. A criminal. A disgrace to his home who wanders through Fae'Gir. The reasons only he knows. What has driven him to those lands is his secret and no one else's. Leaving honour behind, pride, loyalty...

Now, accompanied by a sword whose edge gleams hungry for blood, and with a cloak of secrets that drag him relentlessly, Yuri Akabane seems to seek something in those cursed lands.

And woe to those who cross his path.

Woe to those who feed his sword.

Woe... to everyone.
