Murtagh, the Deathsworn

Mistery, deceit, and blood.

Little is known about Murtagh's past, as he keeps his origins shrouded in secrecy. It is rumoured that he was born into servitude to the Triad, raised from a young age to become a skilled assassin and spy.

The dark magic that the triad has practised on him has made him age slower than regular humans, though not as a slowly as half elves.

As an agent of the Triad, Murtagh carries out high-risk missions, including espionage, assassination, and sabotage. His loyalty to the Triad is unwavering, and he will go to any lengths to ensure their will is carried out.

Given the clandestine nature of his role, his name is seldom uttered, yet when it does escape the lips, it heralds impending doom.
