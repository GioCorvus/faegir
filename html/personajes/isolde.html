Isolde

Isolde, the elder of two sisters, an inseparable duo along with her younger sister, Inara. In the vast stage of the Lands of Dawns, Isolde, hidden among the shadows and far from Gideon's inquisition, carries with her an innate gift: magic. However, her heart harbors no dark intentions or desire for power; her only ambition is to provide a safe haven for her younger sister to grow in peace.

Even as a witch in a world that condemns her ability, she chooses the Lands of Dawns as her home, rejecting the darkness that envelops the Zenith, where magic is accepted but also corrupted. Her sacrifice lies in giving up a place where she could live without hiding her gift, all in order to protect Inara from the dangers lurking in the shadows. Isolde seeks no harm for the kingdom; her only battle is against the stigma that follows her, all for the sake of a safe corner where the dawn's light illuminates her younger sister's path.

Personality

Isolde is a determined and protective woman. Her personality is woven with cunning and tenderness, driven by the desire to provide a safe haven for her younger sister, Inara. Although she hides her magical gift, Isolde longs for a world where magic is accepted without fear or prejudice.

Her fraternal devotion is the beacon that guides Isolde's decisions. Her heart beats with a nobility and kindness that seek a balance between magic and security. But one thing is clear: Not a single trace of that tenderness would remain if Inara were in danger.
