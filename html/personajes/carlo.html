Carlo

In the remote domains of the Dark Zenith, where magic and darkness intertwine their destinies, emerges the enigmatic figure of Carlo. Known as the "Wanderer," this traveler personifies the intrigue and power hidden behind the walls of this nation.

Rumors frequently link him to the capital of the kingdom. It is said that Carlo wanders through dimly lit alleys and dark corners, as if he were in tune with the mystical forces pulsating in the heart of the city. His presence has become an integral part of the urban fabric, an invisible thread that connects the alleyways with the secrets whispered in the elite's halls.

As a frequent visitor to the capital, Carlo has left behind a trail of conjectures and speculations. Citizens gossip about his connection to the powers that govern the Zenith, suggesting that his influence can alter the course of events unfolding between the highest towers and passageways.

Despite his enigmatic aura, Carlo seems to have the ability to traverse both the upper echelons and the marginalized alike. His presence manifests with an aura of deep whispers and dancing shadows, as if the kingdom itself recognizes his arrival as a trigger for hidden secrets.

Personalidad

'I seek the answers that the light would never reveal to me' - Carlo, the Wanderer.

Carlo's personality is as enigmatic as his presence. He possesses a calmness bordering on the immutable, as if the currents of life could barely touch the surface of his being. His deep gaze, always slightly veiled, seems to contain centuries of knowledge and unfathomable experiences.

Carlo is a silent observer, always attentive to the nuances and shadows dancing around him. This reflective attitude is combined with infinite patience, as if he were waiting for the inevitable outcome of the threads of destiny he himself has woven. In his presence, there is a stillness that contrasts with the turbulence of the dark reality he inhabits.

Despite his somber aura, Carlo shows astute intelligence and remarkable strategic ability. His words, few but measured, reveal deep thought and acute understanding of the intricate workings of the Dark Zenith Realm. He is reserved about his own motives and objectives, revealing only what is necessary to maintain the veil of mystery that surrounds him.

While his presence may inspire fear, he also exudes a magnetic charisma that attracts those who seek answers or guidance in the darkness. Some see him as a wise counselor, capable of unraveling hidden truths, while others fear the power that seems to lie beneath the surface of his being. However, none of them ever manage to gather enough courage to approach his figure.

Story

Chapter 1: Hunger.

The life in the suburbs was never easy.

Carlo remembered the imposing walls of the suburbs. The scent of iron, dampness, and blood had permeated his nose, a fragrance he still carried with him with every step. He always slid through the alleys as if they were chapters of a ancient tale that only he could read. The shadows became his faithful friends, and each step resonated with the stealthy dance of his footsteps on the worn pavement.

He was nothing more than a child when he began to read the whispers of the winds between the crumbling buildings, interpreting the lament of a city that forgot its most disadvantaged children. The nights, tinted with a blanket of stars, became his refuge, and his thoughts, the voices in his head, his only companions...

The nights, tinted with a blanket of stars, became his refuge. The moon, witness to his clandestine journeys, cast silver flashes upon his elusive figure. The scars of childhood became elongated shadows on the walls, reminding him of his resilience to adversity.

Carlo, from a young age, among the alleys that curved like mazes, found the hardest but most invaluable lesson: survival. Every encounter with life's harshness had woven another thread into his destiny, forming the tapestry of an orphaned child who learned to defy the darkness, not fearing it, but using it as his own protective veil.

Thus, amid the whisper of moonless nights and the distant echo of a city that seemed to forget its lost children, Carlo, the Wanderer, emerged as a narrator of his own destiny, writing chapters of resistance on the pages of the Dark Zenith.

...but, in the shadow of his gaze, burned an insatiable hunger, not for bread or water, but for something darker and ethereal. A voracious appetite that devoured complacency, an unquenchable thirst that absorbed the shadows of the world to nourish its unfathomable thirst for knowledge and power. In the tangle of his scars, Carlo's hunger became an ominous echo, a flame that burned in the deepest recesses of his being, a prelude to a destiny whose contours were still blurred in the fabric of his story.
