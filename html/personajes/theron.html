Theron, the Grim

Not everything needs an explanation. Not all villains require a tragic backstory to give depth to their motivations. No, some are simply rotten. Mad in the head. Some are born with such darkness in their hearts that the only justification they need is "just because," because they want to.

Theron is one of them. He's a person whose past doesn't matter. Only his actions and his present mark his path. He is the leader of the most secretive and darkest organization that works directly under the orders of the Dark Triad. It is said that he is the most ruthless and unpleasant killer to have ever set foot in Fae'Gir. He is an expert torturer. A diligent student of the dark arts, and the creator of all those perfect experiments, assassins, and spies who work for him, and for the triad.

His gaze is steeped in hatred, in blood, and in darkness. His intentions are always the most twisted, and, on top of all, he possesses one cursed weapon. A dagger that is said to have been forged by a primordial evil. A twisted dagger that, with just one cut, is capable of producing the sharpest and most unbearable pain one can imagine.

He is a bastard, and he does everything possible to live up to the definition.
