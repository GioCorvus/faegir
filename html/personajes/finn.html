Finn

Finnigan was always a quiet shadow. Never the strongest, nor the most fearsome by looks, but what he lacked in brute force, he makes up for in precision. A strategist, a tactician, a man who sees five steps ahead before others take their first. He has a mind built for survival, honed in the alleys of a city that chewed up the weak and left them to rot.

He was once a street rat, one that learned early that power was not just about strength, it was about knowing when to strike and when to wait. Now, he is the Seascourge Pirate's anchor, the one who tempers Viridian’s recklessness with cold logic, who sees threats before they arise. He is swift and efficient, wielding twin daggers with grace. Where Viridian commands like a storm, Finn rules the quiet spaces in between, making sure the ship sails smooth, the crew stays sharp, and no enemy gets too close.

But loyalty is a dangerous thing, isnt't it?. Viridian saved him once, pulled him from a life where he was nothing, and gave him purpose. Whether Finn follows out of faith, debt, or fear of losing the only family he’s ever known... even he isn’t sure.
