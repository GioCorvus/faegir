Barion

He used to fight for the Dawnlads. A good soldier, he was. Kept his head down, did the job. Then someone pinned a crime on him, something bad enough to turn old brothers-in-arms into people who’d rather see him dead. He didn’t stick around to hang for it, though. Now he keeps to the quiet roads, watches every back but his own, and moves like a man with no home to go back to. Not much for words, but steady when it counts.
