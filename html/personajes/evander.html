Evander, the Hunter

Evander is a vampire who has broken the ties that bound him to the sinister triad of the realm. His story is marked by tragedy, when the unjust death of his beloved led him to abandon the shadows that once embraced him. Now, Evander wanders among the shadows of Cenit, dedicating his existence to a singular cause: hunting the creatures of the night that lurk in the same grounds he once called home. It is said that he maintained a close relationship with the highest echelon of the Ebony Crown, but nevertheless, it is also said that after a series of events, which caused the death of his wife, he turned his back on the alliance he held with 'those above'.

His heart, enveloped in the melancholy of loss, has become a crucible of determination and revenge. Evander, renegade of his own vampiric nature, has risen as a relentless hunter, pursuing those who feed on darkness and threaten to plunge Cenit into an even deeper abyss. With every step, he carries the weight of his past and the insatiable thirst for justice, like a specter defying the shadows that once enveloped him.

Personality

His presence evokes a dark elegance and a latent ferocity, manifesting the inherent complexity of his vampiric nature. Melancholy and disdain permeate his being, born from the tragic loss that drove him to abandon the shadows of his past.

Evander stands as an unwavering hunter of nocturnal creatures, imbued with unyielding determination and a keen sense of justice... Or vengeance. His methods, shadowy yet just, reflect an internal morality that defies the shadows that linger in his former home. However, his soul bears the scars of an unforgettable past, and the melancholy in his eyes reveals the weight of eternal loneliness.

In confrontation, he displays unmatched skill and a thirst for vengeance that burns like infernal fire. Although he has left behind the camaraderie of the Cenit triad, he cannot fully escape his own vampiric nature. He is a hunter of nocturnal creatures burdened with the persistent echo of a past that never fades away.
