Naevys

Born into servitude to the Triad, Naevys was raised from a young age to become a skilled assassin and spy for her masters.

Like other agents of the Triad, Naevys carries out high-risk missions, including espionage, assassination, and sabotage. Due to her upbringing , her loyalty to the Triad is unwavering. She is willing to sabotage the work of others, including those she grew up with, to make sure that she gets ahead of them.

She will do anything to complete her mission, and she doesn't care who she hurts in the process.
