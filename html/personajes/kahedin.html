Kahedin

Kahedin's world was gray. Not white. Not black.

The son, along with his twin brother Tristan, of a noble alchemist who died during the Dawn War. The young scholar was always drawn to the hunger for knowledge and all that could make a common being stand out from the rest.

Joining the Order of Alchemists, and following the more empirical branch, Kahedin studies the art of alchemy, the science that seeks to eclipse magic with seals, runes and purity.

Even so, his hunger for knowledge will make him discover that his heart harbors something much more special than all the letters that his books narrate: In his heart dwells the same magic that Dawnlands seeks to eradicate.

Thus, the seed of doubt is sown in his heart: How correct are the Dawnlands' dogmas, which the common human admires so much?
