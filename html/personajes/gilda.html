Gilda

A woman with a map of the stars tattoed on her body, tracing her skin. Once a cartographer for nobles and merchants, she sailed on ships of wealth and privilege, until she uncovered secrets she was never meant to know. Hunted and marked for death, she vanished into the underworld, trading luxury for the lawless freedom of the sea.

Now, she the navigator of the Seascourge pirates, guiding Viridian's ship, reading the stars like an old friend. Sharp-eyed and sharper-tongued, she can be seeing tracing her tattoos when her mind drifts away, remembering how that fine silk and gold felt on her skin. Yet now she doesnt have to just "earn" it by brushing shoulders. Now she claims it.
