Wild Roses

The Wild Roses are a group of rebels, or as they call themselves, 'adventurous heroes,' who defy the yoke of the Zenith and the conventions imposed by the Land of Dawns.

Leading this eclectic band is Zeph, a charismatic leader whose past is shrouded in mystery, yet he does not hesitate to be the vanguard and guide who seeks not only to leave a mark wherever he goes with his group, but also aims to help anyone in need. However, the leader is very selective. His help sometimes comes with a price, and he decides when and how to offer it.

Joining the Wild Roses is complicated. There are no requirements, but they also do not openly accept members. A good way to earn a place among their ranks is to earn the respect or interest of the leader or some of its members. Without an apparent fixed goal beyond traveling the world, filling their pockets with money, their stomachs with food, and their egos with victories, these rebels are both a nest for outcasts and a supernova that catapults itself into being known.

If anything unites the Wild Roses, it is a shared desire for freedom, an immense urge to trace their own narrative on the tapestry of Fae'Gir, sowing seeds of resistance with every step they take.
