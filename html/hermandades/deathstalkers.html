The Deathstalkers

They are the elite who work directly under the orders of The Dark Triad. There are ten of them, spies, assassins, warriors, knights whose bodies have undergone experiments that have deprived them of the emotions that most make a person "human."

They have all lost their memories: All they know, is each other, loyalty and duty. They are all a tool for one purpose: killing machines.

Their origins are unknown. What led them there is unknown. In fact, it's not even known that they exist: They are a secret organization.

Their leader, Theron, the embodiment of unscrupulousness, is the executor. He receives direct orders from the Triad, and he's the one who has experimented on all the others to turn them into the machines they are.

They have slightly extended longevity, but it's most likely that none will live long enough to put it to the test. Each one has some skill that distinguishes them from the others, and woe to those who cross their path.

Speaking that myth, that legend whose name is "The Deathstalkers," is an omen of bad luck.
