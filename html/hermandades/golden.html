The Golden Order

Where light and hope resist the shadows, the Golden Order arises, the indomitable elite of knights who swear to protect the realm. Led by the fearless Adria, a leader whose courage knows no bounds, the Order stands as the last bastion against the threats lurking in the darkness and the magic that plunges the world into chaos.

But the strength of the Golden Order is not limited solely to swordsmanship; they are also masters in the art of alchemy, a science that competes against magical arts. With alchemy and prayers, the knights of the Golden Order enhance their abilities and counteract the dark arts of sorcery and nocturnal beings. With the blessing of light, they punish their enemies with scorching sacred attacks, making their members an unstoppable force in the war against the Zenith.
