Faction: Northern Marches

The Northern Guard

In the icy and desolate Northern Marches, emerges the Northern Guard, a group of unwavering warriors who tirelessly roam the boundaries of civilization. Their grim task is to safeguard the cities of the Marches from the horrors lurking beyond the Forbidden Sea, facing ghouls, dragons, and other abominable creatures that yearn to plunge the lands into endless plagues and calamities.

These warriors, forged in ice and hardened by adversity, represent the last line of defense against the forces seeking to unleash chaos in the Marches. Armed with ferocity, the Guard confronts plagues and beasts with ruthless determination, keeping the borders resilient against any threat arising from the wild and unknown lands.

Feared for their loyalty to the shadows and their indomitable will, they are the wall that protects the northern cities from destruction. Their unsettling presence and unmatched bravery make them untamed guardians, always ready to face anything that dares to set foot in the snowy northern lands.
