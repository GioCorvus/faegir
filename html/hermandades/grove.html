Sylvan Specters

The Sylvan Specters are a group of druids who protect the ancestral Woodlands. Their connection to the ancient forest magics is profound, but their nature is anything but benign. Instead of being benevolent guardians of nature, the Sylvan Specters employ horrific magics that transform the forests into voracious and vengeful entities.

The forests under the influence of the Sylvan Specters become deadly traps; trees that seem to devour those who dare to enter them, and shadows looming like silent predators. The animals surrounding the Woodlands turn into fierce defenders, attacking with bloody violence anyone who threatens the balance of their sacred domain.

The druids of the Sylvan Specters, in their quest to preserve the Woodlands at all costs, have left behind the romantic image of peaceful nature protectors. Now, they are feared entities, whose dark arts serve as a warning to those who venture too close to the Woodlands they guard.
