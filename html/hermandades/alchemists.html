The Alchemists

Not all alchemists are part of the brotherhood, but everyone in the brotherhood is an alchemist. They are a group of scholars, students, and practitioners eager for alchemy, the weapon that the Land of Dawns has created to combat the magic wielded by the Dark Zenith.

Alchemy is considered a science, the art of transmuting matter by channeling the energy of the surrounding environment. Although it was once considered a myth, Gideon, who was renamed 'The Alchemist' for being the individual who rescued these mystical arts to return them to the world after being lost in the dark annals of the history of the realms of Fae'Gir, immersed himself in the content of ancient tomes and scrolls written in forgotten languages until he achieved his goal of reviving it.

Such a science also encompasses the discipline of creating brews and potions with all kinds of purposes, whether they be healing or some with not-so-benign objectives.

The Brotherhood of Alchemists is recognized as an academy that gathers people from the realm seeking to learn these talents. They host everyone from initiates to some of the most prolific alchemists in the Land's monarch's court. However, even though they facilitate the gathering of such knowledge and skills, many people are blessed with enough talent not to have to rely on or depend on the Brotherhood to become alchemists.
