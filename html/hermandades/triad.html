Faction: Dark Zenith

The Unholy Triad

They say there is no greater evil than the Unholy Triad. Where there were once two, now there are three, and amidst the chaos that reigns in the Dark Zenith, there exists a force, an undeniable power that no one can question. That power is the Triad, a trio of individuals who rule from the Ebony City and have sought to control the continent since the early settlements in Fae'Gir.

They establish the rules that the people must follow. The Triad is composed of a representative of the common races, such as humans, elves, and dwarves; a representative of the creatures of the night that have an important part in the Zenith, such as vampires and cursed beings; and a new and enigmatic figure of whom little is known.

Since the arrival of this mysterious new member, the ambitions of the Triad and, consequently, of the Zenith, have been redirected towards the path of domination, with the ultimate goal of plunging the continent into the same eternal night that consumes the capital.

The gears have begun to move again, and it is only a matter of time before the dark forces reclaim what they consider theirs.
