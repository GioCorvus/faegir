Silver Fang Mercenaries

Where cunning and violence are commonplace, the Silver Fang Mercenaries rise as a force both feared and sought after. Led by Uther, a strategist whose name is whispered with recognition throughout the kingdom's lands, this mercenary company has forged its reputation on the razor-sharp edge of survival.

The Silver Fangs are not mere mercenaries; they are an alliance of unscrupulous individuals, masters of tactics and lethal combat. Their loyalty is bought with coins stained in blood, and their prowess on the battlefield is measured by the number of contracts fulfilled. In a world where death lurks around every corner, the Silver Fangs are the shadow that glides through the darkness, offering their services to the highest bidder and leaving behind a trail of fame and fear.

They deal with both monsters emerging from nightmares and fulfill assignments for individuals, their ability to adapt to any situation being their greatest asset in the grim theater of Fae'Gir. Their work is not the cleanest, nor the prettiest: But they carry it out from various settlements across the empire.
