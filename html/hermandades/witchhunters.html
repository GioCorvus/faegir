Faction: Land of Dawns

The Sacred Flame

Behind the radiant image that the Land of Dawns adopts, there are those who must move in the shadows to bring about the idyllic world they fight for in the westernmost part of Fae'Gir: For a world without magic, those who practice it must be eliminated, and just as not everyone wants to submit to the alchemical rituals that deprive them of their magic to adhere to Gideon's and the Golden Order's dogmas, there is not always time to carry out such acts.

This is where the Sacred Flame comes into play, a brutal group of Witch Hunters that extends not only throughout the Land of Dawns, but also beyond where the edges of such a faction blur. All it takes are whispers and rumors that an individual practices the forbidden magic in the west for one of the ruthless Hunters to investigate such rumors.

From alchemists to simply excellent trackers, the hunters of the Sacred Flame are responsible for countless executions across the realm. Stakes, bonfires, and silver, this group is guided by the motto "In the shadows, we watch. Before darkness rises, hunt witchcraft and prevent malevolence."

Some say they don't always hunt witches, and that many innocents have fallen at their hands. But the truth is, for the Hunter's Order... it's better to prevent than to cure.
