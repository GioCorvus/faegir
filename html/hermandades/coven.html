The Coven

The Coven is a circle that gathers the most powerful and darkest witches of the empire. These sorceresses, cunning and power-hungry, spread throughout the lands of the Zenith, weaving their arcane arts in the shadows and exerting a sinister influence over the empire's fate.

The witches of the Coven practice rituals and sacrifices to maintain their youth and increase their power, using methods as ancient as dark magic itself. Each sorceress of the Coven is a mistress in the forbidden arts, capable of conjuring spells that can unleash chaos and destruction in the wars waged by the Zenith. Their magic, fueled by dark alliances and unfathomable secrets, makes them powerful and fearsome weapons in the hands of those seeking total control over the empire.
