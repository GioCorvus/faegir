About the world

In the darkest corners, where light fades and whispers of darkness fill the air, exists a world where fantasy blends with a twisted reality. It's a place shaped by desperation and greed, where telling good from bad is as hard as seeing through the mist that blankets its unfriendly lands.

Here, cities rise like monuments to decay, their gothic towers piercing the ever-cloudy sky like sharp thorns. Narrow alleys hide secrets, while people, marked by scars inside and out, struggle to get by in a world where betrayal is as common as the constant rain.

Out in the wilds, ancient forests hold ancient secrets, and mountains roar with the fury of forgotten creatures. A conflict simmers, fed by lost souls and reckless ambitions. Heroes and not-so-heroic figures, wrapped in fading morals, walk dark paths seeking power, payback, or a shot at redemption. And all the while, dark magic flows like a sinister river, touching every corner of this fractured land.
