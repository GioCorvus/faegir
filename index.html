About the world

In the deepest shadows, where light fades and whispers of darkness dance in the air, lies a world where fantasy intertwines with the most twisted reality. A realm forged in the forge of desperation and greed, where the lines between good and evil are as blurry as the mist that envelops its inhospitable lands.

In this shadowy stage, cities rise like monuments to decay, their gothic towers soaring like sharp thorns towards a perpetually cloudy sky. Narrow alleys whisper dark secrets, while the inhabitants, marked by physical and emotional scars, struggle to survive in a world where betrayal is as common as the merciless rain that falls.

In the vast wilderness, where ancient forests conceal age-old secrets and mountains rumble with the fury of forgotten beings, a conflict brews that feeds on lost souls and devastating ambitions. Heroes and anti-heroes, shrouded in a cloak of faded morality, tread dark paths in search of power, revenge, or redemption, while dark magic flows like a sinister stream, weaving its influence into every corner of the torn landscape.
